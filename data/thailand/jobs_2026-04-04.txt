Corporate Mental Wellness ConsultantPosition OverviewWe are looking for an experienced psychotherapist. To participate in providing mental health services in the organization (Corporate Wellness Program), emphasizing care for employees in the context of work such as stress, burnout and work-life balance. Work type provides one-on-one mental health counseling (1:1 session) to employees in the organization as a part time job, Monday - Friday. Service is provided in an onsite format according to the customer organization, assessing basic physical and mental wellness problems and giving appropriate advice. Work with the team to develop guidelines for caring for employees in the organization. Qualifications Bachelor's degree or higher in Psychology / Clinical Psychology / related field. Experience in counseling at least 3 years, understanding the work context in the organization (Corporate setting), have good communication and listening skills (Active listening), can work part-time and travel to customer organizations. Salary rate is 1500 baht per day, maximum 6 sessions per day. If you can be a lecturer or join in creating content, you will have extra income according to the session.
